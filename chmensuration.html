CUBOID
Let the length,breadth and height of a cuboid be l,b,h (in cm) respectively
Recall a cuboid has six faces. All are rectangles.
Area of top and bottom faces = (lb+lb) cm2 = 2lb cm2
Area of front and back faces = (lh+lh) cm2 = 2lh cm2

Area of side faces = (bh + bh) cm2 = 2bh cm2
Total surface area = 2lb + 2lh + 2bh
= 2(lb + bh + lh) ANS.
CYLINDER
Let us find the surface area of a cylinder of height h and radius r. To find the area of curved surface (lateral surface), let us do another activity.

1. Take a right circular cylinder of radius r units and height h units as shown in the figure 14.19.

2.Take a strip of paper of width h units and wrap it exactly once around the cylinder.

3. Unfold this strip as shown in the figure 14.20.

We will find that the strip is a rectangle of length 2πr and breadth h.

Therefore,
Area of curved surface of cylinder = Area of paper strip ABCD
= 2πr x h [Area of rectangle = length x breadth]

= 2πrh sq.units

Now, let us find total surface area of cylinder.
Total surface area = Area of the curved surface + 2 x Area of base
= 2πrh + 2 x πr2[Area of circle of radius r = πr2]
2πrh + 2πr2
2πr (r + h)sq. units
